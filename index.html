BMW M3

The BMW M3 is one of the most legendary performance sedans. First introduced in 1985, itâ€™s known for combining sharp handling, powerful engines, and everyday usability. Initially based on the BMW 3 Series, the M3 has evolved through several generations: E30 (1985-1991): The original M3, with a 2.3L four-cylinder engine, was lightweight and track-ready, setting the standard for future M models. E36 (1992-1999): Introduced a 3.0L inline-six, offering more power and refinement while keeping the agile driving experience intact. E46 (2000-2006): Known for its perfect balance, it featured a 3.2L inline-six and became a favorite for enthusiasts due to its handling and power. E90/E92 (2007-2013): The first M3 with a V8 engine (4.0L), it delivered 420 hp and had even more aggressive performance. The BMW M3 continues to be a symbol of driving performance and BMW's motorsport heritage.
